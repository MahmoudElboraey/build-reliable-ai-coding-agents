# Plan: Resilient Systems Poem

**Theme:** A system that bends but does not break — failures absorbed, redundancy quietly doing its job, recovery as a natural rhythm rather than a crisis.

**Rhyme scheme:** AABB (four lines, two rhyming couplets).

**Brief for Builder:**
- Line 1-2 (couplet A): Describe stress or failure hitting the system (load, fault, storm-as-metaphor) — establish tension, rhyme on line endings.
- Line 3-4 (couplet B): Describe the system absorbing/adapting and continuing to run — resolve the tension, rhyme on line endings.
- Tone: calm, technical-but-poetic; avoid clichés like "unbreakable" — favor imagery of bending, rerouting, healing.
- Keep each line roughly 6-10 words for rhythm consistency.
